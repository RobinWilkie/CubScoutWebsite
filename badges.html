BADGES

Artist Activity Badge

Choose a favourite art form or artist to take an active interest in.

It doesn’t have to be painting – it could be pop music, sculpture, theatre,

architecture, break dancing or anything similar.

Athletics Activity Badge

Run through an appropriate warm-up and warm-down

routine using all the main muscle groups

Talk about the safety rules in athletics, particularly throwing and jumping events.

Camper Activity Badge

Camp under canvas as a Scout for at least 15 nights.

Find out what to look for when choosing a campsite and in deciding the best position to pitch tents.

Show how you store food hygienically at camp.

Circus Skills Activity Badge

Find out things about circus life and talk about these with an adult.

Watch at least two circus or street performance events. Talk about what you saw.

Find out things about circus life and talk about these with an adult.

Craft Activity Badge

To achieve this badge you need to make or decorate one or more articles using,

whenever possible, original design ideas.

Cyclist Activity Badge

Use a bicycle that is properly equipped. Keep it in good working order.

Show that you can carry out essential maintenance and repairs

Fire Safety Activity Badge

Be aware of dangers in the home and the fire precautions.

Explain what action should be taken and why,

if you discover a fire has broken out in the home or at camp.

Hill Walker Activity Badge

With others, plan at least five one-day journeys of at least 14km in hilly country.

Learn how to summon help in an emergency.

During the journey, show how you use the map and compass.

Hobbies Activity Badge

Take up a hobby or interest that you do not already have an activity badge for.

Keep a record of your hobby for a period agreed

with a member of your leadership team.

Model Maker Activity Badge

Build a model using a plastic or white metal kit or pre-cast figures.

Design and construct a model from a wood, plastic

or metal construction set, such as Lego or Meccano.
